Welcome To R.I.S.E. Against Discrimination

R.I.S.E. Against Discrimination is a website in which we promote equality and unity in hopes to show others the negative effects of discrimination. We hope to encourage others to stand up and fight against discrimination in today's society. Discrimination is the unjust or prejudicial treatment of different categories of people or things, especially on the grounds of race, age, or sex. Here at R.I.S.E. Against Discrimination we focous on racial discrimination, gender discrimination, and religious discrimination.
