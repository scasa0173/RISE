Gender Discrimination

The Workplace

There is about a 20% wage gap between men and women. For every dollar a man makes, a woman makes $0.80. This is unfair especially when they are doing the exact same job and sometimes women do way more important jobs. For women of different races, the gap is even worse. Hispanic women only make 55% of what white men make. No matter what gender you are, you deserve equal pay because you are putting in just as much hard work as men.

Transgender Discrimination

At least one in five transgender people surveyed report experiencing employment discrimination. In six studies done between 1996 and 2006, 20 to 57% of transgender people said they experienced employment discrimination, including being fired, denied a promotion or harassed. They also experienced some form of housing discrimination, such as being denied a home or apartment because of their gender identity.
